Welcome!

Ready to send a helping paw?

Our organization is committed to helping raccoons in need through a multifaceted approach centered on compassion and expertise. With dedicated volunteers and knowledgeable staff, we provide a safe haven for raccoons facing various challenges, including injury, illness, and displacement. Through our rehabilitation programs, we offer specialized care tailored to the unique needs of each individual raccoon, from medical treatment to enrichment activities aimed at fostering natural behaviors. Additionally, we work closely with wildlife experts and local communities to promote coexistence and raise awareness about the importance of protecting raccoons and their habitats.

Beyond the Mask

Learning how to care for raccoons is an enriching journey that requires dedication, patience, and a deep understanding of these fascinating creatures. From providing a safe and stimulating environment to ensuring a balanced diet and proper medical care, the process of caring for raccoons encompasses a wide range of responsibilities. Educating oneself on their natural behaviors, dietary needs, and social dynamics is crucial for meeting their physical and emotional well-being. Additionally, gaining hands-on experience and seeking guidance from wildlife experts or experienced raccoon caregivers can offer invaluable insights into the nuances of raccoon care. Through commitment and compassion, one can cultivate a rewarding relationship with raccoons and provide them with the love and care they deserve.
