Meet our masked family!


This compassionate group worked tirelessly to create a supportive community where raccoons could find solace and comfort amidst life's challenges. From lending a sympathetic ear to offering warm embraces, every member of the Raccoon Family played a vital role in fostering a sense of belonging and understanding. Through group therapy sessions, playful activities, and heartfelt conversations beneath the moonlit sky, these raccoons provided a safe haven for their peers to express themselves and heal from past traumas. With each act of kindness and every shared moment of laughter, they illuminated the forest with the transformative power of empathy and companionship, proving that together, even the darkest of nights could be brightened by the warmth of friendship.

Meet: Nairobi


Age: 10 Years
Gender: Female
Adoptable?: ✗

Emerged a raccoon named Nairobi, whose cunning and wisdom surpassed all others of her kind. With a keen eye for opportunity and a knack for strategy, Nairobi swiftly rose to the forefront of the raccoon community, earning respect and admiration from her peers. Her leadership was not merely about commanding obedience but fostering unity through collaboration and mutual understanding. Whether it was leading foraging expeditions or mediating disputes, Nairobi's guidance was always marked by fairness and foresight. Under her stewardship, the raccoons thrived, their bonds strengthened, and their territory expanded. In the depths of the forest, Nairobi stood tall, a beacon of leadership, guiding her fellow raccoons towards a brighter future.
